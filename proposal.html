01. Origin

Where This Idea Came From

In my two years at RoomingKos Swanston, I've noticed that while tenants are friendly, there is no strong "RoomingKos family" culture. People live alongside each other, but not with each other. I believe this contributes to lower lease renewal rates ‚Äî tenants who don't feel connected have little reason to stay.

Swanston isn't a new or luxury building, but we offer fair pricing, great locations, and reliable service. To stand out against newer purpose-built competitors, we need a differentiator. I believe that differentiator is community culture ‚Äî and it doesn't have to cost us anything.

02. The Concept

The Solution

RoomingKos Explores is a program of group trips organised exclusively for our tenants. We partner directly with local tour operators to offer discounted group rates on destinations like the Great Ocean Road, Phillip Island, and the Yarra Valley ‚Äî places that are practically unreachable for students without a car.

03. Methodology

Bottom Up, Not Top Down

A lesson from past experience: we once tried to organise a ski trip, and uptake was low. Skiing is expensive and niche ‚Äî it simply didn't match our demographic. We picked the activity and hoped tenants would follow. That top-down approach doesn't work.

RoomingKos Explores flips it. We let the tenants decide. I can build a simple voting website where tenants suggest destinations and upvote each other's ideas. The most popular suggestions rise to the top, and we negotiate the deal with a local operator.
